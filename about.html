What is Ryte Club?

Ryte Club is a collaborative writing startup. It's a platform that connects users to create contents together. To begin with, users are able create stories together. Ryte Club allows users to contribute to a story, one paragraph at a time. Story telling progresses with improvisation with thoughts contributed by its readers.

How does collaboration happen in Ryte Club?

Continuation of thoughts. You start a story writing a paragraph or two. Other users may join you writing and extend the story plot you started. With improvisation and collective creativity, stories capture the collective voice of us while recognizing the individual voices in the stories we tell.

What type of contents can I create in Ryte Club?

i) You can start writing a story, opinion or a sci-fi by just writing one or two paragraphs or as much as you want. You can invite your friends to join you writing by sharing what you started in Facebook and Twitter.

ii) You can find interesting contents that are available in our Homepage. You can choose to extend the contents (i.e. story, opinion, science fiction) that you like and share them in Facebook and Twitter asking for more collaboration.

What is the vision of Ryte Club?

Literature and other creative contents are mostly thought as the product of individual genius. Ryte Club wants to demonstrate this idea that collaboration in internet provides enormous opportunity to create meaningful, beautiful, and creative contents through crowdosurcing the creative potential of millions. So, what Wikipedia has done for factual knowlefge, Ryte Club wants to do the same for creative contents.

What features are currently availbale in Ryte Club?

Story creation and sharing: You are able to create story and share it with your friends in Facebook and Twitter. That way, your friends can see what you created and can contribute to your alive stories.

What other features are coming at Ryte Club?

i) Follow list based on your social circle and most popular users: You will see a list of common friends from Facebook that are already in Ryte Club. You can follow them and their stories. Once they follow you or update the stories that you follow, you will get notified in your Facebook.
ii) A comprehensive text-editor: A comprehensive text-editor with all modern features that makes it easy and simple to write. We promise it will be good.
iii) Sending an invite request to a person to collaborate on your story.
iv) Comment on specific paragraph by paragraph: You'll be able to comment on specific paragraph by your liking, allowing you to what you wanted to specifically say.
